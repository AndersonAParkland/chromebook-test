Chromebook Hardware Verification

1. Audio Output Test (Speakers / Headphones)

Plug in headphones (if testing jack) and click the button. You should hear a LOW beep on the LEFT, then a HIGH beep on the RIGHT.

2. Camera & Microphone Test

Click below to authorize and initialize the webcam and microphone capture.

3. Keyboard, Touchpad, & Touchscreen

ChromeOS has a built-in diagnostic test environment for inputs.

Shortcut: Press Ctrl + Search (Magnifying Glass) + Esc to instantly open the Native Diagnostics App, then click "Input" on the left menu to sweep the keyboard and trackpad.
